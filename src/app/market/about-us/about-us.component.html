What is Pedagogy?

The word "Pedagogy" means the discipline that deals with the theory and practice of teaching. It is pronounced variously, as Ped-uh-gaw-G, Ped-uh-go-G or Ped-uh-gaw-gi.

We pronounce it Ped-uh-go-G.
